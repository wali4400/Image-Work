Lahore

Lahore is a city in Pakistan that is known for its rich history, culture, and festivals. It is the capital of the Punjab province and the second largest city in the country. History Lahore is believed to be around 2,000 years old. It was a colony of the Kangra hill kingdoms and a trade route stop to Delhi. The city was conquered by Mahmoud Ghazni in 1021 AD. The Mughals gave Lahore its famous architecture, including the Badshahi Mosque and the Shalimar Gardens. Culture Lahore is known as the "Cultural Heart of Pakistan". It is home to many arts, festivals, music, and film-making. Lahore is also a center of education, with 80% of Pakistan's books published there. Lahore is known for its rich festivals, including the Festival of Lamps, the National Horse and Cattle Show, and kite-flying competitions. Attractions Lahore is known for its many parks and gardens, earning it the nickname "City of Gardens". Lahore is home to many historical mosques, mausoleums, and forts. Lahore is home to the Lollywood film industry.
